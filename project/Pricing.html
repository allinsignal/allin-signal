Frequently asked

Why only $3 a month?
Because the product is a single scanner with a single output per ticker. There's no premium tier where the "real" data lives — what you see on this page is everything we sell. We'd rather price low and have you stay than price high and churn you out.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. One click in your account settings. No retention call, no email gauntlet. Your access continues through the end of the billing cycle you've already paid for.
Is this for day trading?
No. Allin Signal is a buy-and-hold trend system. Ratings refresh on the weekly close and are designed to be held for months to years. If you trade intraday, this is the wrong tool. See the methodology page for the full positioning.
Is this investment advice?
No. Ratings are mechanical readings from our trend model. Your taxes, risk tolerance, time horizon, and overall financial situation are yours to manage — ideally with a qualified advisor.
Do you offer refunds?
If you subscribed by mistake within the last 7 days and haven't used the member features meaningfully, email allinsignalsupport@gmail.com and we'll refund you. After that, just cancel — no further charges.
What payment methods do you accept?
Card, Apple Pay, and PayPal. Payments are processed by our PCI-compliant partner; we don't store card data on our servers.
